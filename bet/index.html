BET

Brain Extraction Tool (BET) is a part of the FMRIB Software Library from Oxford University. Its specialty lies in extracting brain and skull images from MRI scans. It utilizes robust algorithms for separating brain matter from non-brain matter, and it has been extensively used in the field of neurosurgery.
